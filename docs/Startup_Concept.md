# Startup Concept — SUPERSEDED

> Status: Superseded on 2026-07-02. Kept for historical record, per the
> repository's principle of preserving how ideas evolve.

The project's original framing considered a commercial direction (enterprise
orchestration, managed deployments, private clusters, a scheduler-first startup).

**That framing has been retired.** DII is now a free, open-source, non-commercial
research initiative whose goal is a reliable, uncontrollable floor of open-weight
AI for everyone. There is no business model, token, or paid marketplace in scope.

If a *sustainable* funding path (e.g. optional managed federation for
organizations) is ever worth revisiting, it will be evaluated separately and on
its own merits — it is not a goal and nothing in the architecture depends on it.

See [Why DII Exists](./Why_DII_Exists.md) and the
[Architecture Overview](../architecture/Overview.md) for the current direction.
