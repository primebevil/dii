# Project Vision

Keep a dependable baseline of capable, open-weight AI available to everyone, running on participant-owned hardware, resilient by design, and impossible to revoke from above.

Success is measured by continuity: a reliable floor of useful intelligence that keeps individuals, communities, and organizations functioning even when centralized frontier access is restricted, repriced, or switched off.

The model is Tor. Where Tor is privacy for all, sustained by people who run relays because they believe in it, DII is AI for All, sustained by people who run nodes for the same reason. It is a mission-driven, non-commercial volunteer effort: no business model, no token, no marketplace. You contribute because you want your community to keep its intelligence, not to earn anything (ADR-0005).
