# Contributing

This is a free, open, RFC-style research project. Contributions of research,
architecture, and prototypes are welcome.

- Cite sources.
- Label hypotheses as hypotheses.
- Record experiments and decisions (ADRs), and explain *why*.
- Prefer evidence over assumptions; let evidence change conclusions.
- Keep the reliable-floor mission in view: local-first, open weights, no single
  point of control.
