# Startup Concept (Superseded)

Status: Superseded on 2026-07-02. Kept for the historical record, in keeping with the repository's principle of preserving how ideas evolve.

The project's original framing considered a commercial direction: enterprise orchestration, managed deployments, private clusters, and a scheduler-first startup. That framing has been retired. DII is now a free, open-source, non-commercial research initiative whose goal is a reliable, uncontrollable floor of open-weight AI for everyone. There is no business model, token, or paid marketplace in scope. If a sustainable funding path such as optional managed federation for organizations is ever worth revisiting, it will be evaluated separately and on its own merits. It is not a goal, and nothing in the architecture depends on it.

See [Why DII Exists](./Why_DII_Exists.md) and the [Architecture Overview](../architecture/Overview.md) for the current direction.
