# Manifesto

- AI for All, in the spirit of Tor. Where Tor is privacy for all, this is intelligence for all.
- People are the infrastructure.
- You run a node for the mission, not for money or credit. The reason is that your community keeps its intelligence.
- Intelligence is requested as a capability; the router decides which machine serves it.
- The router is the core contribution: coordination across independent nodes.
- Access is the guarantee, and it is never gated on contribution. Most who benefit will never run a node, the way most who use Tor never run a relay.
- Local-first for those who can self-host; the network carries the rest.
- A reliable floor for everyone matters more than an intermittent ceiling for a few.
- No single point that can be switched off from above.
- Non-commercial by commitment: no business model, no token, no marketplace.
