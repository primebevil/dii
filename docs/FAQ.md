# FAQ

**Q: Is this decentralized AI?**
A: It's an open-source research initiative into keeping capable, open-weight AI
available to everyone on hardware they own — resilient by design and not
switchable-off from above.

**Q: Are you competing with the frontier labs?**
A: No. The goal is a reliable *floor* of useful intelligence, not parity with the
ceiling. Different problem.

**Q: Is this a business? Is there a token?**
A: No and no. It's free and non-commercial, with no token, credit-currency, or
paid marketplace. Contribution earns access, not money.

**Q: How do I benefit from contributing hardware?**
A: Running a node gives you priority when you need to spill work onto the
federation. That access is non-transferable and non-tradable — reciprocity, not
currency.

**Q: Does it work offline?**
A: Yes. Local-first is the whole point. A single node with no network still
delivers capable AI; the network is overflow, never a dependency.

**Q: Why does this matter now?**
A: See [Why DII Exists](./Why_DII_Exists.md) — in June 2026 a frontier model was
switched off worldwide by directive, then switched back on two weeks later. That
proved access to centralized models is revocable at will.
